# Beverage Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This interactive Power BI dashboard analyzes beverage sales performance across different brands, regions, and time periods. It provides business stakeholders with key performance indicators (KPIs) and visual insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Problem

Management wants to monitor sales performance, identify top-performing beverage brands, compare revenue and profit, and understand regional sales trends to improve business planning and resource allocation.

## Dataset

The dataset contains beverage sales transaction data, including:

- Beverage Brand
- Revenue
- Profit
- Quantity Sold
- Sales Date
- Division
- District

## Dashboard KPIs

- **Total Revenue:** 8.20M
- **Total Units Sold:** 16M
- **Average Price per Unit:** 0.48

## Dashboard Features

- Revenue by Beverage Brand
- Revenue vs Profit Comparison
- Brand-wise Revenue Distribution
- Division and District Analysis
- Daily Sales Trend
- Monthly Sales Trend

## Business Questions Answered

- Which beverage brand generates the highest revenue?
- Which brands are the most profitable?
- How do sales change throughout the year?
- Which divisions contribute the most revenue?
- What are the daily sales trends?

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling
## Business Insights

- Coca-Cola generated the highest revenue among all beverage brands.
- Monthly sales increased significantly during the middle and end of the year.
- Revenue and profit varied across beverage brands, indicating differences in product performance.
- Sales performance differed across divisions, highlighting regional opportunities.
## Recommendations

- Increase inventory for top-performing beverage brands.
- Develop marketing campaigns in lower-performing regions.
- Monitor seasonal demand to optimize stock planning.
- Focus on improving profitability for brands with lower margins.
