# 👥 HR Analytics & Employee Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview

This interactive Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive insights into employee demographics, workforce trends, hiring, termination, salaries, and job satisfaction. It helps HR managers monitor workforce performance and support strategic decision-making.
## Business Problem

Human Resource managers need a centralized dashboard to monitor employee statistics, identify workforce trends, analyze employee turnover, and support talent management strategies.
## Dataset

The dataset contains employee information, including:
Employee Details
- Department
- Gender
- Salary
- Hire Date
- Termination Status
- Education Level
- Work-Life Balance
- Job Satisfaction
## Dashboard KPIs

- **Total Employees:** 1.47K
- **Current Employees:** 1K
- **Terminated Employees:** 237
- **Termination Rate:** 16.12%
- **Total Salary:** 10M
## Dashboard Features

- Employee Overview
- Department Filter
- Gender Distribution
- Quarterly Hiring Trend
- Quarterly Termination Trend
- Monthly Salary Trend
- Work-Life Balance Analysis
- Job Satisfaction Analysis
- Education Level Distribution
- Salary Analysis by Gender
- Employment Type Analysis

## Business Questions Answered

- What is the employee turnover rate?
- Which departments have the highest employee count?
- What is the gender distribution?
- How does hiring vary throughout the year?
- Which education level is most common?
- How satisfied are employees with their jobs?
- How does work-life balance vary among employees?
## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling
## Business Insights

- The organization currently employs approximately 1,000 active employees.
- The employee termination rate is around 16%, indicating a relatively stable workforce.
- Most employees hold Bachelor's and Master's degrees.
- Employee job satisfaction is generally high, with many employees reporting high or very high satisfaction.
- Hiring and termination fluctuate throughout the year, suggesting seasonal recruitment and workforce planning.
## Business Recommendations

- Investigate departments with higher termination rates to identify retention opportunities.
- Continue initiatives that support employee satisfaction and work-life balance.
- Monitor hiring and turnover trends to improve workforce planning.
- Use salary and employment-type analyses to support fair compensation and staffing decisions.
