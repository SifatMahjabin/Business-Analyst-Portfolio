# Business Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

A business intelligence dashboard that evaluates sales performance, profitability, product performance, and regional sales using interactive Excel visualizations.

---

## Business Problem

Business managers require an executive dashboard to monitor KPIs and identify sales opportunities across products and regions.

---

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin

---

## Dashboard Features

- Category Analysis
- Regional Sales
- Product Performance
- Monthly Trend
- Interactive Region Filter
- Interactive Month Filter

---

## Excel Skills Used

- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- KPI Cards
- Dashboard Design

---

## Business Insights

- Electronics generated the highest sales.
- Regional performance differed significantly.
- Certain products consistently outperformed others.
- Sales fluctuated throughout the year.

---

## Tools

Microsoft Excel
